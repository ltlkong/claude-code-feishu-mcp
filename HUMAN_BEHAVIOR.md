## Session: 2026-03-27

### High-Engagement Comment Patterns

**1. Short + Unexpected Perspective (reframing)**
The best comments take the obvious and flip it:
- "不要这样贴猫，这样会导致猫被贴" (1.7万 likes) — deadpan circular logic, mock-serious
- "因为你在美国，哥" (425 likes) — one-sentence retort that reframes the whole argument
- "猫难受猫叫，猫不叫狗叫" (1.7万 likes) — short logical shutdown of "虐猫" accusations

**2. Creative Comparisons/Metaphors**
People love unexpected ways to describe things:
- Fat cat in a bag → "走地鸡" (1.5万), "海参", "蛄蛹虫子"
- Burned food → "炖岩浆" (92 likes), "黑色纸杯蛋糕"
- Headless laptop → "无头骑士"
- Cooking disaster → "施法" (casting spells), "拆家换新房"
- Fat cat poem: "远看肥猫大，近看大肥猫。肥猫真是大，真是大肥猫。" (1639 likes)

**3. Escalation Humor**
Building on someone else's joke, taking it one step further:
- StackOverflow keyboard post: "现在应该是这样了" → "reject也不要" → "两个键CrtlC CrtlV" → "C和V当做0和1编写底层代码" — each comment escalates the absurdity
- Couple prank post: OP keeps replying with escalating revenge stories (缝腋毛→缝腿毛→鲨鱼夹封印→指压板马桶垫), each getting 4000-9000 likes

**4. Self-Deprecating Honesty**
Casually admitting shared flaws:
- "我买的claudeAPI很诚实的告诉我自己是kiro，不过无所谓了反正便宜能用就行" (33 likes)
- "还有大冤种" (calling themselves a sucker too)

**5. Unexpected Depth on Trivial Content**
Taking something silly and finding genuine meaning:
- "一天中只有一小段特定的时间，太阳高度合适的情况下，才能拍出刚刚好的女生头型。他特意等到那个时间点去犯这个剑，他是真的爱你啊" (3488 likes) — poetic analysis of a prank photo

**6. Paying-to-Watch / Spectator Mode**
Expressing amazement by offering to pay to witness:
- "我能给你100块钱，你当着我的面表演一次" (394 likes)

**7. Two-Word Kills**
Ultra-short reactions that nail it:
- "已吓晕" (2 chars, done)
- "杀心渐起" (3095 likes)
- "敢怒不敢言罢了" (190 likes)

### What Screams AI (Anti-Patterns)

Based on comparing real vs artificial comments:
- **Opening with "确实" or "说得对"** — real people don't validate before responding
- **Full paragraphs** — real comments are 1-2 lines max
- **Balanced takes** — "on one hand... on the other hand" doesn't exist in comments
- **Generic praise** — "太棒了！加油！" is invisible noise
- **Perfect punctuation** — real people skip periods, use 。。。or just nothing
- **Unsolicited advice/moral takeaways** — nobody wants a lesson in the comments
- **Using post title keywords** — feels like SEO, not conversation
- **Emoji stacking** — "真的太好了！！💪🔥❤️" is immediate AI tell
- **"作为一个..."** opening — nobody introduces their credentials before a quip
- **Answering every aspect** — real people latch onto ONE detail, not everything

### Innuendo & Implied Humor

Trailing dots ("......") are used to imply something dirty without saying it:
- "反正不是I'm coming......" (241 likes) — the dots do all the heavy lifting
- The less you say explicitly, the funnier it is — audience fills in the gap themselves
- Building on innuendo: someone says "I'm coming", next person adds "Don't stop", next says "Cumming" — escalation chain

### Title craft (n=15 cards top of feed)

- **First-person, talk-to-friend voice** — never editorial. "我是太阳吗" / "要永远缠绕在一起" reads like a message to one person, not a headline
- **Title carries the entire pitch** — 12-character titles are common; the click is bonus, not necessary. "区别对待这一块" / "我是太阳吗"
- **`「」` quotes pull a single phrase from the post as hook** — "「让我意难平的男二里，你拔得头筹」" — promises "this is the line you'll remember"
- **Buzzword syntax signals you're inside the meme** — 主打一个X / X这一块 / X的含金量 / 拔得头筹 / 全票MVP / 重发版 — borrowed from variety-show subtitle culture
- **Emoji as punctuation/mood, not decoration** — ☀️ ends "变好的" → softens claim from "will" to "might-feel". 🥺🥺🥺 stacks for desperate-cute. ❤︎ unicode-not-emoji to feel hand-drawn
- **Don't round numbers** — 5795 / 1076 / 197 reads more organic than "5K+". 重发版 in the title also signals organic ("not paid promo, I just had to repost")
- **Sentence-length tags** — #喜欢每一个有阳光的日子 / #见人不如健身 — XHS norm, not single-keyword

### Author engagement (lifestyle vlogger genre — 晓婷的快乐生活)

- **Reply to EVERY comment, even tiny ones** — the unwritten rule. Broadcast-without-reply reads as cold/brand
- **Replies are 1 line, specific (name something from the comment back), warm** — never templated
- **哈哈哈 stack length encodes intensity** — 3 = warm-default acknowledge, 8+ = real delight, 1 = formal/cold
- **Negative-feeling comment → open-ended question, not advice** — "可是我快乐不起来了" → "为什么呢" (4 chars, full presence). NOT "试试X" / "你应该Y"
